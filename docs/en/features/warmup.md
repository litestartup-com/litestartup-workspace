# Warm-up

> Gradually increase your email sending volume to build sender reputation.

Email warm-up helps you establish a good sender reputation with email providers by gradually increasing your sending volume over time. This is essential for new domains or email addresses to avoid being flagged as spam.

Navigate to **Emails > Warm-up** from the sidebar.

> **Note**: Email warm-up is available for paid plans only (Pro and Enterprise).

## Getting Started

1. Go to **Emails > Warm-up** from the sidebar
2. Click **Create Plan** to set up a new warm-up plan
3. Select a domain email to warm up
4. Configure your warm-up strategy
5. Monitor progress

## Creating a Warm-up Plan

### Step 1: Select Domain Email

Choose the domain email address you want to warm up. The email must be set up in **Emails > Emails Setup**.

### Step 2: Configure Plan

| Setting | Description | Range |
|---------|-------------|-------|
| Strategy | Warm-up speed | Conservative, Moderate, Aggressive |
| Target Daily Volume | Maximum daily emails at end of warm-up | 10 - 10,000 |
| Total Days | Duration of the warm-up period | 7 - 90 days |
| Sender Display Name | Name shown in recipient's inbox | Optional |
| Sender Role | Your role/title | Optional |
| Sender Company | Your company name | Optional |

### Step 3: Start Plan

1. Review your settings
2. Click **Create**
3. The warm-up plan begins automatically

## Warm-up Strategies

| Strategy | Description | Best For |
|----------|-------------|----------|
| Conservative | Slow, steady increase | New domains, cautious approach |
| Moderate | Balanced increase (default) | Most use cases |
| Aggressive | Faster volume increase | Experienced senders, time-sensitive |

## Monitoring Progress

### Plan Detail

Click on a warm-up plan to view:

- Current day progress
- Daily sending volume
- Delivery statistics
- Plan status

### Plan Status

| Status | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Active | Warm-up is in progress |
| Paused | Temporarily paused |
| Completed | Warm-up finished successfully |
| Cancelled | Plan was cancelled |

## Managing Plans

### Pause Plan

Temporarily stop a warm-up:

1. Open the plan detail
2. Click **Pause**
3. Resume when ready

### Cancel Plan

Stop a warm-up permanently:

1. Open the plan detail
2. Click **Cancel**
3. Confirm cancellation

## Best Practices

- **Start early** - Begin warm-up well before your planned campaign launch
- **Be patient** - Rushing warm-up can hurt your reputation
- **Monitor deliverability** - Watch for bounces and spam complaints
- **Use moderate strategy** - Recommended for most users
- **One plan per email** - Only one active warm-up plan per email address
