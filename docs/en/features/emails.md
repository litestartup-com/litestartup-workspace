# Mailbox

> Send and receive emails efficiently without code.

## Requirements
- You have added the domain to your account, and the DNS records have been verified.
- You have added a domain email address, such as `support@yourdomain.com`.

## How to use

Go to **Emails > Mailbox** from the sidebar, and you can send or receive emails like gmail:

![Emails](/imgs/litestartup-inbox.png)

## Send an email

Click the `+ Compose` button, and you can send an email, and then you can check the email status in `Sent` page:

![Compose](/imgs/litestartup-compose.png)


## Receive an email

Click the `inbox` tab, and you can view received emails.


## Reply an email

Click the `reply` button, and you can reply to the email.

![Reply](/imgs/litestartup-reply.png)
