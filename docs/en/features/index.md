## Email

Professional email infrastructure for your startup — from business mailboxes to marketing campaigns and transactional delivery.

- **[Workmail](emails.md)** — Business email like Gmail. Set up `support@yourcompany.com` in minutes. Save $72–144/year per team member vs. Google Workspace.
- **[Email Marketing](marketing.md)** — Create campaigns, manage contacts, use templates, track opens & clicks. Unlimited contacts on Pro plan. Pay per email sent, not per contact.
- **[Transactional Email](domain-emails.md)** — Send password resets, order confirmations, and notifications via simple REST API. No SDK lock-in. 5-minute integration.
- **[Templates](templates.md)** — Reusable email templates with dynamic variables and drag-and-drop editing.
- **[Contacts](contacts.md)** — Import, tag, segment, and manage your subscriber list.
- **[Analytics](analytics.md)** — Track opens, clicks, bounces, unsubscribes, and conversions in real time.
- **[Warm-up](warmup.md)** — Build sender reputation automatically before launching campaigns. Land in inbox, not spam.
