We just shipped something we think you'll love: **Campaign Emails via AI Skills**.

Instead of switching to a dashboard to write and send marketing emails, you can now do it all from your code editor — just tell your AI assistant what to send, and it handles the rest.

## How It Works

1. Say "send a campaign to newsletter subscribers about [topic]"
2. Your AI writes the email in Markdown and commits it to your repo
3. LiteStartup syncs, converts to HTML, runs a safety review, and sends

The entire flow is version-controlled, auditable, and fast.

## Why We Built This

Marketing emails shouldn't require context-switching. If you're already in your editor shipping features, you should be able to ship emails too — with the same Git workflow you already use for code and docs.

## What's Supported

- **Tag-based targeting** — pick any contact segment
- **AI safety review** — catches spam signals before sending
- **Full tracking** — opens, clicks, unsubscribes
- **Markdown authoring** — no HTML templates needed

Ready to try it? Just ask your AI: *"Send a campaign to my newsletter subscribers."*
