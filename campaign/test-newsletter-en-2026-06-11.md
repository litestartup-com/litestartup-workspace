Hi there,

We're excited to announce a new feature that makes sending campaign emails effortless — **AI-Powered Campaign Sending** is now live on LiteStartup.

## What's New

You can now write and send marketing campaigns entirely through your AI coding assistant. No need to switch to the dashboard — just tell your AI to "send a campaign to newsletter subscribers" and it handles the rest.

## How It Works

1. **Tell your AI what to send** — describe your campaign naturally, like "announce our new pricing to beta users"
2. **AI writes the email** — your assistant drafts the campaign in Markdown, picks the right contact tag, and commits it to your content repo
3. **Auto-sync & review** — LiteStartup syncs the file, converts it to HTML, runs an AI safety review, and queues it for sending
4. **Delivered** — your contacts receive a polished email, with full open/click tracking

## Why You'll Love It

- **Stay in your editor** — no context-switching to a marketing dashboard
- **Version-controlled campaigns** — every email lives in your Git repo as a Markdown file
- **Safe by default** — AI review catches issues before anything goes out
- **Tag-based targeting** — send to exactly the right audience segment

## Get Started

Make sure your content repo has a `campaign/` directory and your `litestartup.yaml` includes the campaign sync path. Then simply ask your AI assistant:

> "Send a campaign email to my newsletter subscribers about [topic]"

That's it. Your AI handles file creation, Git push, and sync trigger automatically.
